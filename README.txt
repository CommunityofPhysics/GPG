RESEARCH
GitHub repository for the Division of Experimentation, Community of Physics.

*** Code of conduct ***
  1.  This folder and its subfolders contain the codes and computational guidelines relevent to the research projects conducted by Community of Physics. 
  2.  The folder titled "CPG" contains materials from Classical Physics Group only. Similarly, the "GPG" folder contains materials from the Gravitational Physics Group. 
  3.  Files of one group must not be misplaced in another's folder.
  4.  Files must not be created, edited, modified uploaded or deleted without permission from the proper authority. 
  5.  Failure to abide by these rules may result in stringent action. 
  6.  All approved codes from projects are to be uploaded in the Github repository as a standard practice of Community of Physics.
